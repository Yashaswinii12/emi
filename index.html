About EMI Calculator

This EMI Calculator helps users estimate their monthly loan repayment amount based on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

EMI Formula

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)N] / [(1+R)N − 1]

  • P = Loan Amount (Principal)
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100)
  • N = Tenure in Months

Types of Loans

  • Personal Loan
  • Home Loan
  • Education Loan
  • Car Loan

Loan Risk Category

Based on EMI as a percentage of Monthly Income:

  • Safe Loan: EMI ≤ 30%
  • Moderate Loan: EMI 30% – 50%
  • Risky Loan: EMI > 50%
